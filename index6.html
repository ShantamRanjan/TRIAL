This is the fifth step where the audio goes through localisation algorithm

Hyperbolic localization uses the geometry of hyperbolas and Time Difference of Arrival (TDOA) to accurately determine the position of a sound source, like a gunshot, by intersecting hyperbolas from multiple microphone pairs.
